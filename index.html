About Us.

Monie African Braiding has been in business since 2013 and has provided patrons like you with excellent customer service for 21 years. We specialize in all different styles and forms of hair braiding, including short hair. Our hair stylists have spent many years honing their abilities in the art of African hair Braiding. You can trust that your hair will look gorgeous after you visit our salon. You will always be treated like royalty at Monie African Hair Braiding Salon and never have to worry about seating too long to braid your hair. Monie African Braiding has braided musician's hair from California to Utah. Our hair experts are skilled and qualified in the art of African hair Braiding and styling. We will make your hair look great and your hair is in good hands. If you want to have smooth, fast Braiding, gorgeous and impressive hair Braiding, come see our experts today. Our Services.

View some of the services we provide. If you have any questions, call us any time.

Box Braids

Box braids are individual plaits that are usually divided by small squared off parts or boxes.

Twist Braid

Similar to a traditional braid, but the strands are twisted around eachother extremely tightly.

Micro Braid

Micro braids are tiny, delicate braids that are tightly woven into hair, and generally last for several months.

Corn Rows

A style of braiding and plaiting the hair in narrow strips to form geometric patterns on the scalp.

Weaving- Extensions

A hairstyle created by weaving pieces of real or artificial hair into a person's existing hair, typically in order to increase its length or thickness.

Tree Braids

Tree braids are a natural type of hair extension that is a way to use cornrow braiding to add extensions, braids and knots hold the hair extensions in place.
